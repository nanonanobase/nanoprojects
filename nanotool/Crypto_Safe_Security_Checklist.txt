Crypto-Safe Security Checklist

Purpose:
Ensure your Bitcoin wallet and crypto activities are secure.

Essential Steps:

1. Use a hardware wallet – Ledger, Trezor, or equivalent.
2. Enable 2FA – For all wallets and exchanges.
3. Secure seed phrases – Offline storage only.
4. Verify wallet addresses – Double-check every transaction.
5. Separate wallets – Hot wallet vs cold storage.
6. Use multisig for critical funds – Require multiple signatures.
7. Stay updated on scams – Follow crypto security alerts.
8. Check software authenticity – Only download wallets from official sources.
9. Backup regularly – Keep encrypted offline backups.
10. Limit sharing info – Don’t expose balances or holdings publicly.

Optional for agents:
- Can be structured as JSON/YAML for automated verification.
